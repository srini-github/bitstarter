How does it work?

Scenario1: You are a strong social influencer.
You are a person having 100+ contacts on LinkedIn, 200+ connection on Facebook and 300+ followers on Twitter. Congrats! you are a potential strong influencer in the area you are playing. Important thing to note is that business and people playing in your area of interest will be happy to reward you for what value you can add to them, it could range anything from a simple advice to a profitable business opportunity. It is also possible that a lot of advertisers are already trying to use your network and connections without your knowledge. So would it not be great if you can set a formal channel for business and people to connect with you and are ready to reward for the value you bring? OpenDoor will be a platform to provide this service.


Scenario2: You are a significant person - CEO of a company, CTO, a powerful political player, someone with major accomplishment or a celebrity.
You are an important person and have a presence in social network but not necessarily active in social network in what you share and tweet. The fact is a lot of people would be ready to pay to just talk to you based on your accomplishments and position. OpenDoor gives opportunity for people to connect with an important person like you who they value. OpenDoor will be a platform that will allow a significantly important person to choose and directly connect with people who value them and gives opportunity to monetize their value.


Scenario3: You are someone who is in need of a strong influencer who could add value to your business or someone who can help to make a critical decision.
You are working on a marketing campaign and would like to get an expert advice from an important person who you value. You are working on a new product and would like to consult with strong influencers in the area you are working on. You wish to get a well respected person as your mentor for a project and would like talk to that person. OpenDoor provides an opportunity for you to directly knock at the doors of these people you value and you can set the reward price to show your seriousness.


Summary

OpenDoor is a platform that creates opportunity for people to easily connect with people they value. And help people with strong social influence to benefit from what they have achieved. The platform unlocks a lot of possibilities and opportunities creating win-win outcomes.
